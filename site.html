What is the purpose of this page?

This page is meant to teach you about the 4 basic math operations, addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. These 4 operations are essential to know and are used in almost all forms of math. Without knowing these functions it would be impossible to do most math and so it is extremely important to learn them.
