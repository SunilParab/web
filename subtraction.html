What is subtraction?

Subtracting is also very simple, and just like we did for addition, let's start with an example. Sally has 5 apples and then she gives 2 away. How many apples does she have now? If you said 3 you are correct, and you just subtracted. It's just as simple as adding. Just like with adding you could think of each number represeting a number of objects. You write a subtraction equation like this: 5 - 3 = 2, since - is the subtraction symbol, also know as the minus sign (shown at the bottom of the page). Unlike addition, the placement of the numbers in a subtraction equation matter, 5 - 3 is not the same as 3 - 5.
